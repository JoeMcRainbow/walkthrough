checkra1n is a semi-tethered (needs a computer) jailbreak that is unpatchable by Apple.

checkra1n is based on the checkm8 exploit.

In order to jailbreak with checkra1n, you will need:

  • A Mac or Linux computer
  • A USB-A/USB-C to Lightning Cable
  • A jailbreakable iDevice
  • An internet connection
  • About 20 minutes
